TECHMART ELECTRONICS — USER MANUAL & MAINTENANCE GUIDE
=======================================================

SECTION 1: ULTRABOOK PRO 15 & AIR 13 — USER GUIDE
----------------------------------------------------

1.1 KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS (WINDOWS 11)
Win + D          → Show/hide desktop
Win + L          → Lock screen
Win + E          → Open File Explorer
Win + I          → Open Settings
Fn + F1          → Mute/unmute audio
Fn + F2/F3       → Volume down/up
Fn + F4          → Mute microphone
Fn + F5          → Toggle keyboard backlight
Fn + F6/F7       → Screen brightness down/up
Fn + F8          → Toggle display output (internal/external/mirror)
Fn + F10         → Toggle touchpad on/off
Fn + F12         → Toggle TechMart AI Assistant
Ctrl + Shift + Esc → Task Manager

1.2 BATTERY CARE & MAINTENANCE
- Best practice: Keep battery between 20% and 80% for daily use to maximize long-term capacity.
- TechMart Battery Health Mode (TechMart PC Toolkit → Battery → Health Mode): Limits charging to 80% for daily desk use.
- Full charge (100%) is fine for travel days when you need maximum runtime.
- Avoid storing the laptop at 0% or 100% for extended periods.
- Expected battery lifespan: 80% capacity after 1,000 charge cycles.
- Battery replacement service available through TechMart Warranty (see warranty policy).

1.3 DISPLAY CARE
- Use a soft, lint-free microfiber cloth to clean the screen. Lightly dampen with water if needed.
- Never use paper towels, abrasive materials, or household cleaners — these can scratch the anti-reflective coating.
- Avoid pressing hard on the screen.

1.4 PORTS & CONNECTIVITY
UltraBook Pro 15:
  - USB-A 3.2 Gen 2 (10Gbps, up to 900mA): peripherals, USB drives
  - USB-C Thunderbolt 4 (40Gbps, up to 100W PD charging, 8K display output)
  - HDMI 2.1 (4K@120Hz or 8K@30Hz external displays)
  - SD Card Reader (UHS-II)
  - 3.5mm combo headphone/microphone jack

UltraBook Air 13:
  - 2× USB-C (USB 3.2 Gen 2, 10Gbps, up to 65W PD charging)
  - No built-in HDMI or USB-A: use TechMart USB-C Multiport Adapter (sold separately)
  - 3.5mm combo jack

1.5 STORAGE MANAGEMENT
- Check disk usage: Settings → System → Storage
- Free up space: Storage Sense (Settings → System → Storage → Storage Sense)
- External SSD recommended for large media libraries (TechMart-compatible USB-C SSDs available)
- TechMart PC Toolkit → Disk Cleaner for safe junk file removal

1.6 FACTORY RESET (WINDOWS 11)
- Go to Settings → System → Recovery → Reset this PC
- Choose "Keep my files" (reinstalls Windows, removes apps) or "Remove everything" (full reset)
- Full reset takes 1–2 hours. Ensure power is connected.
- All personal data is removed. Backup first via OneDrive, Google Drive, or external drive.

SECTION 5: EARBUDS PRO — CARE & MAINTENANCE
----------------------------------------------

5.1 CLEANING
  - Earbuds: use dry cotton swab to clean speaker mesh and sensors. Never use liquid directly on earbuds.
  - Case: wipe with dry microfiber cloth. For charging contacts, use dry cotton swab.
  - Ear tips: remove and wash with mild soapy water; dry fully before reattaching.
  - Frequency: clean weekly for heavy users.

5.2 EAR TIP FIT
  - Proper fit is essential for optimal ANC and bass response.
  - The right ear tip creates a light seal in the ear canal — not a deep insert.
  - Test: put earbuds in, play music; hold hand over right earbud — if bass noticeably increases, the seal is good. If not, try a different tip size.
  - Sizes included: XS, S, M (pre-installed), L, XL.

5.3 FIRMWARE UPDATES
  - Updates via TechMart Audio app (Android/iOS) → Earbuds → Check for Updates
  - Keep earbuds in case during update; ensure case is at least 30% charged.

SECTION 6: GENERAL SAFETY INFORMATION
---------------------------------------

6.1 BATTERY SAFETY
  - Do not disassemble, crush, puncture, or expose any TechMart device to fire or extreme heat.
  - If a battery swells (visible bulge), stop using immediately, do not charge, and contact TechMart support.
  - Do not use third-party chargers with unapproved wattage — risk of overheating or battery damage.
  - Charge in well-ventilated areas; do not charge under pillows or blankets.

6.2 ELECTROMAGNETIC INTERFERENCE
  - Keep TechMart devices at least 15cm from pacemakers. Consult a physician if you have a pacemaker.
  - Power off devices on aircraft unless airline explicitly permits Airplane Mode use.
  - SmartWatch: not suitable for use near MRI machines; remove before any MRI examination.

6.3 ERGONOMICS
  - Laptops: use at eye level on a desk. Use an external keyboard and mouse if laptop is raised.
  - Smartphones and tablets: take breaks every 30 minutes to reduce eye strain and repetitive strain.
  - Earbuds: keep volume below 85dB for prolonged use. The TechMart Audio app shows volume level in dB.
  - TechMart devices support EU Volume Limitation at 85dB for European users.

6.4 OPERATING ENVIRONMENT
  - Storage temperature: -20°C to 45°C (-4°F to 113°F)
  - Operating temperature: 0°C to 35°C (32°F to 95°F)
  - Humidity: 5%–95% non-condensing
  - Altitude: Up to 5,000m (16,400 ft)
