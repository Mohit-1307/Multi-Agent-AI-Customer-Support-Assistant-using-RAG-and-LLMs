GENERAL QUESTIONS
-----------------
Q: What products does TechMart sell?
A: TechMart offers a wide range of consumer electronics including laptops, smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, wireless earbuds, smart home speakers, and related accessories. We also offer TechMart Care subscription plans for extended protection and priority support.

Q: Where does TechMart ship?
A: TechMart ships to all 50 US states, Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, France, Australia, Japan, and over 40 other countries. International shipping options and costs vary by destination.

Q: Does TechMart have physical stores?
A: TechMart currently operates as a direct-to-consumer online retailer. We do not have brick-and-mortar stores, but we partner with select authorized retailers in major cities. Check our website store locator for partner locations near you.

Q: How do I create a TechMart account?
A: Visit www.techmartelectronics.com and click "Sign Up" in the top right corner. You will need a valid email address and password. Account creation is free and gives you access to order tracking, purchase history, and exclusive member discounts.

Q: Can I place an order without an account?
A: Yes. You can check out as a guest. However, having an account lets you track orders, access purchase history, manage warranties, and receive personalized offers.

Q: Does TechMart offer price matching?
A: Yes. TechMart offers price matching for identical products (same model, color, and specifications) sold by major authorized retailers. The competitor must have the item in stock at the lower price at the time of the request. Contact support within 14 days of your purchase.

Q: What payment methods does TechMart accept?
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and TechMart Gift Cards. We also offer 0% APR financing through Affirm for orders over $300 (subject to credit approval).

Q: Does TechMart charge sales tax?
A: Sales tax is collected in states where TechMart has a legal obligation to do so. The applicable tax is displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

Q: Are my payment details secure?
A: Yes. TechMart uses 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions. We are PCI-DSS Level 1 certified. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Log into your TechMart account and visit the "Orders" section. You will find real-time tracking for all orders. A tracking email is also sent once your order ships.

Q: Can I cancel or modify an order?
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement by calling 1-800-TECHMART or using live chat. After 1 hour, the order enters fulfillment and cannot be cancelled. You would need to initiate a return once the item arrives.

Q: Does TechMart sell refurbished products?
A: Yes. TechMart Certified Refurbished products are professionally inspected, tested, and cleaned. They come with a 90-day warranty. These are clearly labeled "Certified Refurbished" on the product page.

Q: Do you offer student or education discounts?
A: Yes. TechMart offers a 10% discount on most products for verified students and educators. Visit our Education Store at www.techmartelectronics.com/education and verify your status with a valid .edu email or school ID.

Q: Does TechMart have a loyalty or rewards program?
A: Yes. TechMart Rewards earns you 1 point for every $1 spent. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases. TechMart Care subscribers earn double points on all purchases.

Q: Can I buy a gift card?
A: Yes. TechMart Gift Cards are available in denominations from $25 to $500. They can be purchased online and sent digitally or as a physical card. Gift cards never expire.

ACCOUNT & SECURITY
------------------
Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email, and you will receive a password reset link within 5 minutes. The link expires in 24 hours. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Q: How do I update my email address or billing information?
A: Log into your account, navigate to "Account Settings," and update your details there. For security reasons, changing your primary email address requires verification from both the old and new email addresses.

Q: Is my personal data shared with third parties?
A: TechMart does not sell your personal data to third parties. We share limited data with shipping partners and payment processors only as required to fulfill your order. Our full privacy policy is available at www.techmartelectronics.com/privacy.

Q: How do I delete my TechMart account?
A: Contact our support team via email at privacy@techmartelectronics.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request within 30 days in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

PROMOTIONS & DEALS
------------------
Q: How do I use a promo code?
A: Enter the promo code in the "Discount Code" field during checkout. Only one promo code can be applied per order. Promo codes cannot be combined with other offers unless stated otherwise.

Q: Where can I find TechMart deals and promotions?
A: Sign up for the TechMart newsletter, follow us on social media (@TechMartElec), and visit the "Deals" section of our website. We run major sales on Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Back to School, and other seasonal events.

Q: My promo code is not working. What should I do?
A: Verify that the code is entered correctly (codes are case-sensitive), that it has not expired, and that your cart meets the minimum purchase requirement. If the code is valid and still fails, contact support and a team member will apply the discount manually.
