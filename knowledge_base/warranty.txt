TECHMART ELECTRONICS — WARRANTY POLICY
=======================================
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. LIMITED WARRANTY OVERVIEW
------------------------------
TechMart Electronics ("TechMart") warrants that its products will be free from defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and service conditions for the duration specified below. This warranty is extended to the original purchaser only and is non-transferable.

2. WARRANTY PERIODS BY PRODUCT CATEGORY
-----------------------------------------
a) Laptops and Desktop Computers
   Standard Warranty: 1 year from the date of purchase
   TechMart Care Basic: 2 years total (standard + 1 additional year)
   TechMart Care Pro: 3 years total (standard + 2 additional years)

b) Smartphones
   Standard Warranty: 1 year from the date of purchase
   TechMart Care: Extends to 2 or 3 years depending on plan tier

c) Tablets
   Standard Warranty: 1 year from the date of purchase

d) Smartwatches and Wearables
   Standard Warranty: 1 year from the date of purchase

e) Wireless Earbuds and Headphones
   Standard Warranty: 1 year from the date of purchase (does not cover ear tips and cushions, which are consumable parts)

f) Smart Home Speakers and HomeHub Devices
   Standard Warranty: 1 year from the date of purchase

g) Accessories (cables, chargers, cases, adapters)
   Standard Warranty: 90 days from the date of purchase

h) Certified Refurbished Products
   Warranty: 90 days from the date of purchase

3. WHAT IS COVERED
-------------------
The TechMart limited warranty covers:
  - Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  - Hardware failures under normal use conditions
  - Defective screens, batteries, or ports that fail within the warranty period under normal use
  - Internal component failures not caused by external damage

4. WHAT IS NOT COVERED (WARRANTY EXCLUSIONS)
----------------------------------------------
The warranty does NOT cover:
  - Physical damage caused by drops, impacts, crushing, or bending
  - Liquid or moisture damage
  - Damage resulting from unauthorized modifications or repairs
  - Normal wear and tear (scratches, fading, discoloration of external surfaces)
  - Consumable parts (batteries when capacity reduces below 80% within warranty period is covered; ear tips, stylus tips are not)
  - Damage from power surges or improper voltage
  - Cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality
  - Products with removed or altered serial numbers
  - Damage caused by operating the product outside the permitted or intended uses described in the documentation
  - Software issues, viruses, or corrupted operating system
  - Loss or theft

NOTE: TechMart Care Pro subscribers receive accidental damage coverage (screen cracks, liquid damage) for up to 2 incidents per subscription year, subject to a service fee of $49 (smartphones) or $79 (laptops).

5. HOW TO MAKE A WARRANTY CLAIM
---------------------------------
Step 1: Gather the following:
  - Proof of purchase (order confirmation email, receipt, or account purchase history)
  - Product serial number (found on the device, original packaging, or account device list)
  - Description and photos/video of the defect or issue

Step 2: Contact TechMart:
  - Online: Log into your account, go to "Devices," select the product, and click "Request Warranty Service."
  - Phone: Call 1-800-TECHMART and select "Warranty Service"
  - Email: warranty@techmartelectronics.com

Step 3: Diagnosis and Resolution:
  - A TechMart technician will review your claim within 2 business days.
  - If the issue can be resolved remotely (e.g., software issue), instructions will be provided.
  - If a hardware repair or replacement is required, TechMart will issue a prepaid shipping label to send your device to a TechMart Service Center.

Step 4: Repair or Replacement:
  - Repair: Estimated turnaround is 7–14 business days.
  - Replacement: If the device cannot be repaired, TechMart will replace it with the same or a functionally equivalent model (refurbished or new, at TechMart's discretion).
  - Data backup before shipping is the customer's sole responsibility. TechMart is not responsible for data loss during repair.

6. OUT-OF-WARRANTY SERVICE
----------------------------
For devices outside the warranty period, TechMart offers paid repair services. Diagnostic fees start at $39.99 (applied toward the repair cost if you proceed). Contact warranty@techmartelectronics.com for a service quote.
