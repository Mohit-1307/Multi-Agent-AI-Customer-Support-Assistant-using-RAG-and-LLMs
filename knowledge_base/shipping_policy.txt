TECHMART ELECTRONICS — SHIPPING POLICY
=======================================
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. SHIPPING OVERVIEW
---------------------
TechMart ships to addresses across the United States, Canada, UK, Europe, Australia, and select countries in Asia and Latin America. All orders are processed and dispatched from our fulfillment centers in San Jose, CA and Nashville, TN.

2. DOMESTIC SHIPPING OPTIONS (UNITED STATES)
---------------------------------------------
a) Standard Shipping (Free on orders over $50; $5.99 otherwise)
   Carrier: USPS, FedEx Ground, or UPS Ground
   Estimated Delivery: 5–7 business days after dispatch
   Available: All 48 contiguous states; Alaska and Hawaii add 2–3 extra days.

b) Expedited Shipping ($12.99)
   Carrier: FedEx 2Day or UPS 2-Day
   Estimated Delivery: 2–3 business days after dispatch

c) Express Overnight Shipping ($24.99)
   Carrier: FedEx First Overnight or UPS Next Day Air
   Estimated Delivery: Next business day if ordered before 2 PM EST
   Not available for P.O. Boxes, APO/FPO addresses, or Alaska/Hawaii.

d) Same-Day Delivery ($19.99)
   Available in select metro areas (New York City, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Chicago, Miami).
   Orders must be placed before 11 AM local time.
   Visit the product page to check same-day availability at your address.

e) Free Shipping Threshold
   Orders of $50 or more automatically qualify for free standard shipping. The $50 threshold applies after discounts and before taxes.

3. ORDER PROCESSING TIME
--------------------------
Orders placed on business days (Monday–Friday, excluding US federal holidays) before 3 PM EST are typically dispatched the same day. Orders placed after 3 PM EST, on weekends, or on federal holidays are dispatched the next business day.

During peak seasons (Black Friday, Cyber Monday, holiday shopping periods), processing may take an additional 1–2 business days. TechMart will communicate any delays via email.

4. INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
---------------------------
a) Canada
   Flat rate: $14.99 CAD
   Estimated Delivery: 7–14 business days
   Duties and taxes: May apply at the border; responsibility of the recipient.

b) United Kingdom & European Union
   Flat rate: $19.99 USD
   Estimated Delivery: 10–18 business days
   VAT/customs fees: Recipient is responsible for any applicable duties.

c) Australia & New Zealand
   Flat rate: $24.99 USD
   Estimated Delivery: 12–21 business days

d) Japan & South Korea
   Flat rate: $22.99 USD
   Estimated Delivery: 10–16 business days

e) Rest of World (available countries listed on checkout page)
   Flat rate: $29.99 USD
   Estimated Delivery: 15–30 business days

TechMart is not responsible for delays caused by customs inspections, local postal services, or force majeure events.

5. ITEMS RESTRICTED FOR INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
------------------------------------------------
Certain items may be restricted from international export due to local regulations or product certifications. These include:
  - Some battery-powered devices (subject to airline cargo restrictions)
  - Products with region-specific software
  - High-capacity lithium battery packs
If an item cannot be shipped to your country, this will be indicated on the product page.

6. SHIPPING CONFIRMATION & TRACKING
-------------------------------------
A shipping confirmation email with a tracking number is sent once your order is dispatched. Tracking is available via:
  - Your TechMart account under "Orders"
  - The carrier's website using the provided tracking number
  - SMS tracking alerts (opt-in during checkout)

Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to appear on the carrier's website after dispatch.

7. SIGNATURE REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------
Orders over $500 require an adult signature upon delivery. If no one is available to sign, the carrier will leave a delivery notice and attempt redelivery up to 3 times. After 3 failed attempts, the package is held at the nearest carrier facility for 5 business days before being returned to TechMart.

8. UNDELIVERABLE PACKAGES
---------------------------
If a package is returned to TechMart due to an incorrect address, failed delivery attempts, or refusal, we will contact you to arrange reshipment (additional shipping charges may apply) or issue a refund minus the original shipping cost.

Please ensure your shipping address is correct at checkout. Address changes cannot be made once an order is dispatched.

9. LOST OR STOLEN PACKAGES
-----------------------------
If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you have not received the package:
  - Wait 24 hours as carriers sometimes mark packages delivered early.
  - Check with neighbors and building management.
  - Contact TechMart support within 10 days of the delivery date.
  - File a police report for stolen packages if needed.
  - TechMart will open a carrier investigation and, if confirmed lost or stolen, issue a replacement or full refund.

10. SHIPPING DURING HOLIDAYS
------------------------------
TechMart observes the following US holidays and will not dispatch orders on these days: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day.

During the holiday season (November 15 – January 5), order volumes increase and standard shipping estimates may be extended by 1–3 business days. Expedited and express options remain the most reliable during this period.
