TECHMART ELECTRONICS — REFUND & RETURN POLICY
==============================================
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Last Reviewed: October 1, 2024

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
TechMart Electronics is committed to your complete satisfaction. If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, we offer a straightforward return and refund process subject to the terms below.

2. STANDARD RETURN WINDOW
--------------------------
Most items purchased from TechMart can be returned within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. Items must be:
  - In original, unused condition
  - In original packaging with all accessories, manuals, and documentation included
  - Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase
  - Free from physical damage, liquid damage, or signs of misuse

3. PRODUCT-SPECIFIC POLICIES
------------------------------
a) Laptops, Desktops, and Tablets
   Return within 30 days. Must be factory reset before return. Any data remaining on the device is the customer's responsibility.

b) Smartphones
   Return within 30 days. Must be unlocked from carrier lock and iCloud/Google account removed (device unlinked). The IMEI must not be blacklisted.

c) Software, Games, and Digital Downloads
   Non-returnable once the seal is broken or the digital key has been redeemed, unless the product is defective. If defective, contact support within 14 days of purchase.

d) Opened Accessories (cables, chargers, cases, screen protectors)
   Returnable within 30 days if defective. Non-defective opened accessories may be subject to a 15% restocking fee at TechMart's discretion.

e) Certified Refurbished Products
   Return within 30 days. Same conditions as new products apply.

f) TechMart Care Subscription Plans
   Cancel anytime. If cancelled within 30 days of subscription start, a full refund of the first payment is issued. After 30 days, the subscription remains active until the end of the billing cycle; no partial refunds are issued.

g) Gift Cards
   Gift cards are non-refundable and non-exchangeable for cash.

h) Special Order or Customized Products
   Non-returnable unless the item arrives damaged or defective.

4. EXCEPTIONS – ITEMS THAT CANNOT BE RETURNED
-----------------------------------------------
The following items are non-returnable:
  - Opened earbuds, headphones, or in-ear products (for hygiene reasons)
  - Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance"
  - Hazardous materials (e.g., certain batteries)
  - Products with a removed or altered serial number
  - Items purchased through unauthorized sellers

5. HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
-----------------------------
Step 1: Log into your TechMart account and navigate to "Orders."
Step 2: Select the item you wish to return and click "Start Return."
Step 3: Choose your reason for return and preferred resolution (refund or exchange).
Step 4: Print the prepaid return shipping label provided (available for US returns only).
Step 5: Pack the item securely in its original packaging and drop it off at any UPS or FedEx location.
Step 6: Once the item is received and inspected at our returns center, the refund or exchange will be processed.

Alternatively, contact support at 1-800-TECHMART or support@techmartelectronics.com to initiate your return.

6. REFUND PROCESSING TIMELINE
-------------------------------
  - Credit/Debit Card: 5–7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.
  - PayPal: 3–5 business days.
  - TechMart Gift Card: 1–2 business days (refund issued to original gift card or a new gift card).
  - Affirm Financing: TechMart will issue the refund to Affirm. Affirm will adjust your loan balance within 3–10 business days. Interest paid is non-refundable.
  - Original shipping charges are not refunded unless the return is due to TechMart's error or a defective product.

7. DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
--------------------------------
If your item arrives damaged or is defective upon receipt:
  - Contact support within 7 days of delivery.
  - Provide photos or video evidence of the damage or defect.
  - TechMart will arrange a prepaid return shipping label and expedite the replacement or refund.
  - For defects discovered after 7 days but within the warranty period, see the Warranty Policy document.

8. EXCHANGES
--------------
TechMart offers exchanges for the same product (different size, color, or variant) or for a different product of equal or lesser value. For exchanges to a higher-priced item, the customer pays the price difference.

Exchange requests follow the same process as returns. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

9. INTERNATIONAL RETURNS
--------------------------
Customers outside the US are responsible for all return shipping costs. TechMart does not provide prepaid return labels for international orders. Customs duties and taxes paid at the time of delivery are non-refundable by TechMart. Contact international support at global@techmartelectronics.com.

10. RETURN FRAUD & POLICY ABUSE
----------------------------------
TechMart monitors return activity. Customers who exhibit patterns of excessive or fraudulent returns may have their return privileges suspended or their accounts restricted.
