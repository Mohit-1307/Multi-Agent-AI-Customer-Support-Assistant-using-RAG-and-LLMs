TECHMART ELECTRONICS — INSTALLATION GUIDE & TROUBLESHOOTING
===========================================================

SECTION A: TECHMART ULTRABOOK PRO 15 / ULTRABOOK AIR 13
---------------------------------------------------------

A1. INITIAL SETUP
- Unbox the laptop and remove all protective films and packing materials.
- Connect the included USB-C charger. Charge to at least 20% before first use.
- Press and hold the Power button for 2 seconds to turn on.
- Follow the Windows 11 setup wizard (language, Wi-Fi, Microsoft account sign-in or local account).
- Register your product at www.techmartelectronics.com/register within 30 days to activate warranty.

A2. DRIVER AND SOFTWARE INSTALLATION
- TechMart recommends running Windows Update immediately after setup.
- Go to Settings → Windows Update → Check for Updates.
- Install TechMart PC Toolkit from www.techmartelectronics.com/downloads for:
  * Firmware updates
  * Battery optimization settings
  * TechMart Control Center (fan profiles, keyboard backlight, display calibration)
- Restart after all updates complete.

A3. COMMON LAPTOP ISSUES & FIXES
- Laptop won't turn on: Ensure charger is fully plugged in. Hold Power button for 10 seconds (force shutdown), then press Power again. If still unresponsive, contact support.
- Black screen on startup: Press and hold Power for 10 seconds to force restart. If recurring, boot into Safe Mode (hold Shift during restart > Troubleshoot > Startup Settings).
- Overheating / loud fan: Ensure vents (bottom and back) are not blocked. Use on flat, hard surfaces, not bed/sofa. Run TechMart Control Center and select "Performance" or "Balanced" mode.
- Wi-Fi not connecting: Restart router. Forget Wi-Fi network and reconnect. Update Wi-Fi drivers via Device Manager → Network Adapters.
- Keyboard backlight not working: Press Fn + F5 to toggle backlight. In TechMart Control Center, customize brightness levels.
- Battery draining fast: Set Power Mode to "Balanced." Lower screen brightness. Disable background apps in Settings → Apps → Startup.
- Low disk space: Open Storage Settings to identify large files. Use TechMart PC Toolkit > "Disk Cleaner." Consider external SSD (USB-C, compatible with all TechMart laptops).

SECTION B: TECHMART SMARTPHONE X14 / X14 PRO
-----------------------------------------------

B1. SIM & INITIAL SETUP
- Insert nano SIM into the SIM tray (side port) using the included ejector pin.
- Power on by holding the Power button (right side) for 3 seconds.
- Follow Android setup: select language, insert SIM, connect to Wi-Fi, sign in with Google account.
- Restore from backup (Google account or old device backup) or set up as new.
- Register device at www.techmartelectronics.com/register.

B2. FACE UNLOCK & FINGERPRINT SETUP
- Go to Settings → Security → Biometrics.
- Add fingerprint: place finger on the in-display fingerprint sensor (center bottom of screen).
- Add face data: face the camera in good lighting.
- Recommended: set up both for convenience and fallback.

B3. TRANSFERRING DATA FROM OLD PHONE
- Android to TechMart: Use "Switch to Android" app on old phone or Google Backup (Settings → Google → Backup).
- iPhone to TechMart: Use the "Move to Android" app (available on App Store) or export contacts via iCloud CSV.

B4. COMMON SMARTPHONE ISSUES & FIXES
- Phone won't turn on: Hold Power + Volume Down for 10 seconds. If unresponsive, charge for 30 minutes then retry.
- Touchscreen unresponsive: Clean screen. Remove screen protector if third-party (may interfere). Force restart (Power + Volume Down, 10 seconds).
- Apps crashing: Go to Settings → Apps → [app name] → Force Stop, then Clear Cache. Update app. If still crashing, reinstall.
- Mobile data not working: Check Settings → Network & Internet → Mobile Network → ensure Mobile Data is On and APN settings match your carrier. Toggle Airplane Mode on/off.
- Overheating: Remove case, stop background-intensive apps, avoid direct sunlight. If persistent, contact support.
- Battery draining: Settings → Battery → Battery Usage to identify draining apps. Reduce screen brightness. Disable 5G if not needed (saves battery).
- Camera not focusing: Clean lens with soft cloth. Switch between cameras (front/rear). Restart Camera app. If still blurry, contact warranty support.
- Forgot PIN/Pattern: After 5 failed attempts, use "Forgot PIN" option and sign in with Google account. If Google account is also inaccessible, contact support for account recovery.

B5. BACKUP & DATA SAFETY
- Google backup: Settings → Google → Backup → Back Up Now.
- Photos: Enable Google Photos backup (automatic when connected to Wi-Fi).
- Contacts: Sync to Google account (Settings → Accounts → Google → Sync Contacts).

SECTION C: TECHMART TABPRO 11
--------------------------------

C1. INITIAL SETUP
- Power on by holding the top button.
- Follow Android tablet setup. Connect to Wi-Fi and Google account.
- Attach TechMart Smart Keyboard Folio (sold separately) via Smart Connector on the back bottom edge.
- Pair TechMart Stylus Pro 2: attach magnetically to the right edge to charge and pair.

C2. KEYBOARD FOLIO SETUP
- Simply connect via Smart Connector; no Bluetooth pairing needed.
- Adjust backlight: Fn + F5.
- If trackpad is not responding, go to Settings → Connected Devices → Keyboard and toggle off/on.

C3. STYLUS PRO 2 SETUP
- First use: attach to tablet magnetic edge for 15 minutes to charge.
- LED indicator: White = paired; Amber = charging; Red = low battery.
- Customize stylus shortcuts: Settings → Connected Devices → Stylus Pro 2 → Button actions.

SECTION D: TECHMART SMARTWATCH SERIES 3
-----------------------------------------

D1. SETUP & PAIRING
- Charge watch using the included magnetic charging cable. Charge for 1 hour before first use.
- Power on: press and hold the Crown button (right side) for 3 seconds.
- Download "TechMart Watch" app on your smartphone (Android 10+ or iOS 16+).
- Open app → Add device → Select SmartWatch Series 3. Enable Bluetooth on phone.
- Follow pairing instructions in the app.

D2. HEALTH DATA SETUP
- Grant TechMart Watch app permission to Health/Activity data during setup.
- Set up emergency contacts for SOS/fall detection in app settings.
- For ECG: Settings in Watch app → Health → ECG → agree to terms.
- For SpO2: Settings → Health → SpO2 → enable background monitoring.

D3. COMMON WATCH ISSUES
- Watch not pairing: Ensure Bluetooth is enabled. Force close TechMart Watch app and retry. Restart watch (hold Crown 10 seconds).
- Watch face not displaying: Enable Always-On Display in watch Settings → Display.
- Inaccurate step count: Wear watch snugly (one finger gap from wrist). Enable Wrist Detection in app.
- Notifications not arriving: In TechMart Watch app → Notifications → ensure permissions granted for each desired app.

SECTION E: TECHMART TRUE WIRELESS EARBUDS PRO
-----------------------------------------------

E1. FIRST-TIME PAIRING
- Remove earbuds from case (this begins pairing mode automatically).
- On your phone, go to Bluetooth settings → pair "TechMart Earbuds Pro."
- Subsequent connections: earbuds auto-connect to the last paired device when removed from case.

E2. MULTIPOINT (TWO DEVICES)
- To connect to a second device: in Bluetooth settings of the second device, select "TechMart Earbuds Pro."
- Earbuds will auto-switch between the two most recently connected devices.

E3. TOUCH CONTROLS
- Left earbud: single tap = play/pause; double tap = volume down; triple tap = previous track; press and hold = toggle ANC/Transparency
- Right earbud: single tap = play/pause; double tap = volume up; triple tap = next track; press and hold = voice assistant
- Customize controls in TechMart Audio app (Android/iOS).

E4. ANC & TRANSPARENCY MODE
- Press and hold either earbud for 1 second to cycle: ANC → Transparency → Off.
- ANC is best in steady-state noise environments (airplane, train). Transparency mode lets environmental sound through for situational awareness.

SECTION F: TECHMART HOMEHUB SPEAKER
-------------------------------------

F1. INITIAL SETUP
- Plug in the HomeHub using the included power adapter.
- Download "TechMart Home" app on your smartphone.
- Open app → Add Device → HomeHub Speaker. Follow on-screen instructions to connect to your Wi-Fi.
- Say "Hey Aria" to activate TechMart's AI assistant.

F2. SMART HOME INTEGRATION
- In TechMart Home app → Integrations → add Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit, or Philips Hue.
- The HomeHub can control lights, thermostats, smart locks, and other Matter-compatible devices.

F3. MULTI-ROOM AUDIO
- In TechMart Home app → Rooms → assign HomeHub to a room. Add multiple HomeHub speakers to create a multi-room audio group.

F4. PRIVACY & MICROPHONE
- Press the physical Mute button on the top to disable microphones (LED turns Red).
- View microphone activity log in TechMart Home app → Privacy → Microphone History.
